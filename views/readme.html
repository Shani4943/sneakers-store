How to Operate:

  • Contacting Support: Use the `/contact` page to send inquiries or issues to customer support. Upon submission, you'll be redirected to `/contact-success` to confirm that your message has been received.
  • Purchasing a Gift Card: Visit `/buy-gift-card` to purchase a gift card, customize the amount, add a personal message, and send it directly to someone. After completing the purchase, you'll be redirected to `giftcardThankYou.ejs` to confirm the transaction.
  • Reviewing Terms and Conditions: Click on `/terms` to review the terms and conditions of the website.
  • Adding to Cart/Wishlist: Click the "Add to Wishlist" button on product pages to save items you're interested in.

Challenges Faced:

  • Implementing security measures, specifically for preventing DOS attacks, proved difficult. At one point, downloading a folder disrupted the logic and functionality of the entire site
  • I dedicated a lot of effort to designing the pages. My goal was to create layouts that catered to the unique needs of each page while maintaining a consistent design across the entire site. Balancing these two aspects was particularly challenging
